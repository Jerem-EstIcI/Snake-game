Snake Menu

Game Objective of 1 Player Mode

Your goal: The object of Snake game is to control a snake moving around a 2D playing field, making it eat food to make it grow while avoiding hitting the walls or the tail of the snake itself.
The snake continuously moves in a given direction, and the player can change this direction using the arrow keys on the keyboard.
The bigger the snake gets, the more difficult it becomes to control, as it takes up more space large part of the screen and its tail becomes an obstacle to avoid.
The game ends when the snake hits a wall or its own tail, and the goal is to get the highest possible score by eating as many possible food before losing.

Goal of the game of 2 player mode (local)

In addition to the goal of 1 Player mode your goal is to get more "score" points than your opponent.
